╔════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║          KNIGHT ANIMATION - MANUAL CONTROLS                    ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                ║
║  ANIMATION TRIGGERS:                                           ║
║                                                                ║
║    [1] → Idle      (standing, breathing)                      ║
║    [2] → Attack    (sword swing)                              ║
║    [3] → Hit       (take damage)                              ║
║    [4] → Death     (fall down)                                ║
║                                                                ║
║  MOVEMENT:                                                     ║
║                                                                ║
║    [→] → Walk Right  (150 px/s)                               ║
║    [←] → Walk Left   (150 px/s)                               ║
║                                                                ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  HOW IT WORKS:                                                 ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                ║
║  • Knight faces the direction of last movement                 ║
║  • Number keys use current facing direction                    ║
║  • Attack/Hit/Death play once, then return to Idle            ║
║  • Walk animations loop while arrow key held                   ║
║  • Release arrow key → returns to Idle                         ║
║                                                                ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  ANIMATION SPEEDS:                                             ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                ║
║    Idle:    4 FPS  (0.5s per cycle)                           ║
║    Walk:    6 FPS  (0.67s per cycle)                          ║
║    Attack:  8 FPS  (0.5s total)                               ║
║    Hit:     6 FPS  (0.33s total)                              ║
║    Death:   4 FPS  (0.5s total)                               ║
║                                                                ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║  EXAMPLE TEST SEQUENCE:                                        ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                ║
║  1. Press [1] → See idle animation                            ║
║  2. Hold  [→] → Walk right                                    ║
║  3. Press [2] → Attack (facing right)                         ║
║  4. Press [3] → Get hit (facing right)                        ║
║  5. Press [4] → Death (facing right)                          ║
║  6. Press [1] → Reset to idle                                 ║
║  7. Hold  [←] → Walk left                                     ║
║  8. Press [2] → Attack (facing left)                          ║
║                                                                ║
